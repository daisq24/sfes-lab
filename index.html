Manifesto · 实验室宣言

空间不说话，但它记得一切。 Space does not speak — yet it remembers everything.

人类文明从来不只栖居于语言符号之中。城市与建筑——从紫禁城的轴线到徽州的马头墙——是人类智能以物质形式书写的史诗，是语言与社会结构之外，另一套沉默而持久的文明语法。

Civilization has never dwelt in language alone. The built environment — axes, thresholds, courtyards, ruins — is another grammar of intelligence, slower than speech and harder to forget. This lab asks what that grammar means now, as machine agents learn to navigate space without inhabiting it, and as attention capital empties the places where memory lives.

—— 戴诗琪 / DAI Shiqi  ·  独立观察者视角

Proposal · In Progress

Spatial Familiarity as an Emergent State. 一份关于空间如何作用于具身智能体的基准提案

Current embodied AI research measures how agents act on space — navigating, mapping, manipulating. This proposal identifies the opposite gap: no benchmark measures how space acts on agents.

在人类的空间认知中，熟悉感并非经验的累积，而是一种内部状态的动态转变——它改变注意力模式、行为效率、异常敏感度与情感反应。SFES 提案，正是试图让机器对"空间"也拥有一次被动地、缓慢地、被改变的机会。
